Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the EAUR–QuickBooks Integration Platform (hereafter referred to as “the Integration”) handles, stores, and protects financial and personal information shared between EAUR’s Management Information System (MIS), QuickBooks Online, and authorized payment partners.

Information We Collect

The Integration collects limited but essential data to ensure accurate synchronization between systems. This includes:

  • Student identification and academic profile data (non-sensitive).
  • Payment records such as fees, receipts, and transaction references.
  • Audit and reconciliation data from QuickBooks Online.
  • System logs required for performance and fraud analysis.

How We Use Collected Data

All collected data is used exclusively to enhance operational efficiency and maintain accurate financial records. Specifically, the Integration uses your data to:

  • Automate synchronization between EAUR’s MIS and QuickBooks Online.
  • Provide real-time financial dashboards and reconciliation reports.
  • Validate and confirm USSD payments through connected gateways.
  • Ensure full audit compliance with institutional and national standards.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share necessary financial and transactional data with:

  • QuickBooks Online (Intuit Inc.) for accounting and reporting purposes.
  • Authorized payment partners such as School Gear and Urubuto Pay.
  • EAUR’s internal finance and compliance teams.

No personal data is shared with third parties for marketing or unrelated purposes.

Data Security

Security is a top priority. The Integration employs:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit and at rest.
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) to restrict unauthorized usage.
  • Fraud detection algorithms for duplicate payment prevention.
  • Regular security audits aligned with QuickBooks data protection standards.

Compliance and Audit

The Integration adheres to local data protection regulations and QuickBooks developer compliance policies. All financial transactions maintain a full audit trail and timestamped reconciliation record for transparency.

Data Retention

Financial and audit data are retained for the legally required duration or as mandated by EAUR’s internal financial policies. Upon expiration, data is securely deleted from all connected systems.

Your Rights

Users whose data passes through the Integration may:

  • Request correction of inaccurate financial or identity data.
  • Request access to data shared with QuickBooks.
  • Request deletion of financial records, subject to compliance policies.

Updates to this Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect new regulatory requirements or changes in the integration framework. The latest version will always be available on the EAUR–QuickBooks Integration portal.
